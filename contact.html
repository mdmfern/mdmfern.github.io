BMW M1000RR

The BMW M1000RR is a superbike that is homologated for racing. It was perfected in the wind tunnel and further developed on the circuit. It has optimized aerodynamics and uses carbon, resulting in a significantly higher top speed of 314 km/h. Its engine and chassis remain as powerful and direct as ever. The M RR stands for absolute performance and exclusivity down to the last detail. Just the typical M.

CBR 1000RRR SP

The CBR1000RR-R Fireblade SP is a high-performance motorcycle from Honda that is designed for track use. It is powered by a 999cc liquid-cooled inline-four engine that produces 160 kW of power. The bike also features Öhlins Smart Electronic Control (S-EC) suspension, Brembo master cylinder, brake lever, front and rear calipers and standard-fit quick shifter. The CBR1000RR-R Fireblade SP is a very capable motorcycle that is sure to please even the most demanding riders.

APRILIA RSV4

The Aprilia RSV4 is a high-performance superbike that has been competing at the highest levels of motorcycle racing since its introduction in 2009. It is powered by a 1,099cc V4 engine that produces over 200 horsepower. The RSV4 is equipped with a sophisticated suite of electronic rider aids, including traction control, ABS, and wheelie control. It is a very fast and capable motorcycle, but it is also very expensive.
